My Personal Interests

I've always been a fan of technology. Since I was a kid, I've immersed myself in it- usually by sitting in front of a computer screen (much to the dismay of my parents). It wasn't all bad, though. I'm often the guy people go to when they need help fixing a printer or a laptop, and I've gotten pretty good at it. Now I work in IT, but I hope to learn a lot about coding and put those skills to good use.

Italian food is, in my opinion, the best kind of food there is. I'm not sure what possessed Italy's finest culnerary creators to perfect the mountains of different pastas and pizzas of the world, but they deserve our respect and praise. Of course, Americans have done their thing and American-ized that section of the food industry which was probably better left untouched. Despite that, there's still a lot of really good italian food around here and I've made myself hungry thinking about it.

I'm slowly becoming intrigued by the prospect of Game Design. The truth is I can't stand working for people and I'd love to develop some software or game I can sell to people on my own. Whether or not it's successful, I've made that one of my primary ambitions for now. I think working in the gaming industry is often a thankless job, so I'd rather get a college degree under my belt and make game development a bit of a side project.
